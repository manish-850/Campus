Frequently Asked
Questions.

Resolving campus hurdles instantly.

REF#
STATED PROBLEM / QUERY
SEGMENT
01
How do I track my complaint status once submitted?
General
You can use the 'Track Status' feature on the home page. Simply enter the unique Tracking ID generated at the time of submission (e.g., #CC-8821).
02
What is the turnaround time for electricity-related issues?
Hostel
Urgent electricity issues are typically assigned to the maintenance department within 30 minutes and resolved within 4-6 hours.
03
Can I cancel a canteen order after placing it?
Canteen
Orders can only be cancelled within 2 minutes of placing them. After that, the kitchen starts preparation and cancellations are not permitted.
04
How do I report a missing item in the hostel?
Security
Please use the 'New Complaint' form and select 'Security' as the category. Provide your Room No. and a detailed description of the item.
05
Is there any provision for emergency medical support?
Support
Yes, contact details for the Medical Officer are available in the 'Support' section. For immediate emergencies, contact the main security gate.
06
What should I do if my Wi-Fi is not connecting in the room?
Technical
Submit a complaint under the 'IT Support' category. Ensure you mention your MAC address and Room Number for faster troubleshooting.
07
How do I pay for my canteen order via COD?
Payment
Select 'Cash on Delivery' at the payment modal. You must pay the exact amount to the delivery executive upon receiving your food.
08
Are hostel visitors allowed during late hours?
Hostel
Visitors are strictly allowed only until 8:00 PM. Any late-night visits require prior written permission from the Chief Warden.
09
Where can I find the Director's official email?
Admin
The official contact directory for all college heads, including the Director, is listed in the 'Support' page of this portal.
10
Can I update my room number in a submitted complaint?
General
Currently, submitted complaints cannot be edited. Please raise a new request or add a comment once the Executive contacts you.
